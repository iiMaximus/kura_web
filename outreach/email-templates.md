# Kura Outreach Email Templates

Last updated: 2026-05-15

Use these for one-to-one outreach only. Customize the first sentence, keep claims accurate, and stop contacting anyone who says no.

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, fill in:

- Recipient name and site.
- The exact page, episode, newsletter, or resource you are referencing.
- Why Kura is relevant to their audience.
- The best destination URL.
- One proof point, screenshot, or short demo link.
- A clear, low-friction ask.

## Claims To Keep Out Of Outreach

- "Guaranteed safe."
- "Doctor-approved" unless formally true and documented.
- "Detects every allergen" or "works on every product."
- "FDA approved" or similar regulatory endorsement.
- "Clean" as an absolute claim. Prefer "ingredient-conscious" or "beauty ingredient scanning."
