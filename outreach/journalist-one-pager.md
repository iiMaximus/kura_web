## Short Description

Lychee is a food and beauty scanner that helps shoppers scan product labels and understand ingredients in plain language before they buy.

## What Lychee Does

Lychee helps users scan food products, cosmetics, skincare, makeup, haircare, sunscreen, fragrance, and personal care products. The app turns ingredient labels into plain-language context so shoppers can review products faster.

For food, Lychee focuses on ingredient labels, additives, allergens, processing cues, and product transparency. For cosmetics and beauty, Lychee helps explain ingredient lists, ingredient roles, and common concern categories.

Lychee is an educational label-reading tool. It is not medical, nutrition, allergy, or dermatology advice.

## Why It Matters

Consumers increasingly read labels across categories. The same shopper may check a snack label, sunscreen ingredient list, moisturizer formula, and packaged drink in one trip. Lychee reflects that behavior by supporting both grocery and beauty label scanning in one app.

## Story Angles

- Label literacy is expanding from food into beauty and personal care.
- Shoppers want fast product context at the point of purchase.
- AI-assisted explanations can make labels easier to understand, but responsible tools need clear boundaries.
- Beauty ingredient scanning needs nuance because concentration, exposure, product type, and personal sensitivity matter.
- Food label scanning can support better questions about ingredients, additives, allergens, and processing cues.

## Who It Is For

- Ingredient-conscious shoppers.
- Families who already read labels carefully.
- Beauty and skincare shoppers comparing formulas.
- People who want faster product context before buying.
- Journalists and educators covering consumer transparency, shopping tools, food labels, or beauty ingredient literacy.
