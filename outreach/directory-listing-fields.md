## Short Listing Description

## Medium Listing Description

Lychee helps ingredient-conscious shoppers understand product labels faster. Scan food products, cosmetics, skincare, makeup, haircare, sunscreen, fragrance, and personal care items, then review ingredient information in plain language. Lychee covers food label context such as ingredients, additives, allergens, and processing cues, plus cosmetics and beauty ingredient lists. It is educational and not medical, nutrition, allergy, or dermatology advice.

## Long Listing Description

Lychee is a mobile scanner app for people who read labels. It helps shoppers scan food products and beauty products, then understand ingredient information in plain language before they buy or use a product.

For food, Lychee helps users review ingredients, additives, allergens, processing cues, and product transparency signals. For cosmetics and beauty, Lychee helps users review ingredient lists, ingredient roles, and common concern categories across products such as skincare, makeup, haircare, sunscreen, fragrance, deodorant, and personal care.

Lychee is designed for ingredient-conscious shoppers who want faster product context in the grocery aisle, beauty aisle, pharmacy, or online cart. It is not medical advice, an allergy guarantee, a diet plan, or dermatology advice. Users should still check the current product label, manufacturer information, and qualified professional guidance for personal health decisions.
