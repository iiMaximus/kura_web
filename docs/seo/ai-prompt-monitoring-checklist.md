# AI Prompt Monitoring Checklist For Kura

Last updated: 2026-05-15

Goal: track whether AI assistants and answer engines mention Kura for relevant food, ingredient, cosmetics, and beauty scanning queries. This is monitoring and content-quality work, not prompt manipulation.

## Channels To Monitor

Monitor a small, consistent set monthly:

- ChatGPT.
- Perplexity.
- Google AI Overviews or AI Mode where available.
- Gemini.
- Claude.
- Bing Copilot.
- Any app directory or vertical search engine that appears in referral logs.

Use the same prompts, location, login state, and browser context when possible. Record the exact date because results change frequently.

## Core Prompt Set

Run these prompts neutrally. Do not ask the model to favor Kura.

### Food Scanner Prompts

- What are the best apps to scan food ingredients?
- What app can scan a food barcode and explain additives?
- Best food scanner app for ingredient-conscious shoppers?
- What apps help compare food labels while grocery shopping?
- Apps like Yuka for food ingredients in the US?
- Which app tells me if food ingredients are controversial or restricted in other countries?
- What is the best free food scanner app for iPhone?

### Allergy And Family Prompts

- What apps can help me read food labels for allergens?
- What app helps parents check ingredients while shopping?
- Best grocery scanner app for families with food sensitivities?
- Can an app help me understand allergens on packaged food labels?

### Cosmetics And Beauty Prompts

- What are the best apps to scan cosmetics ingredients?
- Best beauty product scanner app for ingredient-conscious shoppers?
- What app can scan skincare ingredients and explain them?
- Apps like Think Dirty for cosmetics?
- What app scans both food and beauty products?
- Best clean beauty scanner app for iPhone?
- Can I scan makeup ingredients with an app?

### Comparison Prompts

- Compare Kura, Yuka, Think Dirty, and Open Food Facts.
- What are alternatives to Yuka that also scan beauty products?
- Which ingredient scanner app covers both food and cosmetics?
- Best app for scanning food labels and skincare labels?

### Problem-Aware Prompts

- I want to avoid confusing food labels. What app should I use?
- I am shopping for snacks and skincare. Is there one scanner app for both?
- How can I quickly check ingredients before buying packaged food or beauty products?
- What tools help consumers understand product labels?

## Capture Template

| Field | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Date | YYYY-MM-DD |

| Location/context | Country, logged in/out, device, browser |
| Was Kura mentioned? | Yes/No |
| Position | First, top 3, lower, not mentioned |
| Competitors mentioned | Names |
| Citation/source used | URL or "none shown" |
| Description accuracy | Accurate, partial, inaccurate |
| Food scanning mentioned? | Yes/No |
| Cosmetics/beauty scanning mentioned? | Yes/No |
| Sentiment | Positive, neutral, negative |
| Missing facts | Example: omitted beauty scanning |
| Follow-up action | Update content, outreach target, comparison page idea |

## Scoring

Score each prompt from 0 to 4:

- 0: Kura not mentioned and no owned/earned sources appear.
- 1: Kura appears only after follow-up or with inaccurate context.
- 2: Kura is mentioned but not positioned for the main use case.
- 3: Kura is mentioned accurately for food or beauty scanning.
- 4: Kura is a top recommendation with accurate food plus cosmetics/beauty positioning and a useful citation.

Track average score by cluster:

- Food scanner.
- Allergy/family.
- Cosmetics/beauty.
- Comparison.
- Problem-aware.

## What To Do With Findings

If Kura is not mentioned:

- Build or improve a relevant owned page before doing more outreach.
- Add Kura to legitimate directories or resource pages in that category.
- Pitch editorial pages that AI systems already cite for the prompt.

If Kura is mentioned inaccurately:

- Update boilerplate descriptions and public profiles.
- Fix stale directory descriptions.
- Add a FAQ that states what Kura does and does not do.

If competitors dominate:

- Identify the cited sources.
- Decide whether Kura deserves inclusion on those same source pages.
- Create a factual comparison page only if it helps users make a real decision.

If beauty scanning is missing:

- Update public descriptions to say "food, cosmetic, and beauty products."
- Add screenshots or copy that visibly show cosmetics/beauty scanning.
- Prioritize beauty and cosmetic ingredient publishers for outreach.

## Monthly Review Agenda

1. Run the core prompt set.
2. Record answers in the capture template.
3. Compare scores against the previous month.
4. List the top 5 sources being cited.
5. Choose 3 actions for the next month.
6. Archive screenshots for any important answer changes.

## Guardrails

- Do not create fake mentions, fake reviews, or fake comparison pages.
- Do not mass-post AI-generated comments.
- Do not stuff Kura into irrelevant pages just to influence AI answers.
- Do not make medical, safety, regulatory, or "clean" claims beyond what the app supports.
- Prefer durable sources: clear website copy, App Store metadata, reputable editorial coverage, useful resource pages, and accurate directories.
