## 100-Word Description

Lychee is a food and beauty scanner app for people who want faster clarity on product labels. Shoppers can scan food products, cosmetics, skincare, makeup, haircare, sunscreen, fragrance, and personal care items, then review ingredient information in plain language. For food, Lychee focuses on ingredients, additives, allergens, processing cues, and product transparency. For cosmetics and beauty, Lychee helps explain ingredient roles and common concern categories. Lychee is built as an educational label-reading tool, not a diet plan, allergy guarantee, medical device, or dermatology advice.

## 200-Word Press Boilerplate

Lychee is a mobile scanner app built for people who read labels. It helps shoppers scan food products, cosmetics, skincare, makeup, haircare, sunscreen, fragrance, and personal care items, then understand ingredient information in plain language before they buy or use a product.

For food, Lychee helps users review ingredient labels, additives, allergens, processing cues, and product transparency signals. For cosmetics and beauty, Lychee helps users make sense of ingredient lists, ingredient roles, and common concern categories across products such as moisturizers, cleansers, serums, makeup, deodorant, fragrance, and sunscreen.

Lychee is designed for ingredient-conscious shoppers, families, beauty shoppers, and anyone who wants faster label context without turning every purchase into a research project. The app is an educational tool and does not replace the product label, manufacturer information, or professional medical, nutrition, allergy, or dermatology guidance.

## Proof Points To Verify Before Publishing

- Supported platforms and regions.
- Whether Android is publicly available in the target country.
- Whether core scanning is free at the time of publication.
- Current screenshots for food scans and cosmetics/beauty scans.
- Any claims about AI features, databases, health scores, heavy metal risk, or regulatory references.
- Founder name, company name, launch date, user count, and funding status.

## Phrases To Avoid

- "Guaranteed safe."
- "Detects every allergen."
- "Doctor-approved."
- "Dermatologist-approved."
- "FDA-approved."
- "Replaces a dietitian, allergist, doctor, or dermatologist."
- "The healthiest food scanner."
- "The cleanest beauty scanner."
- "Works on every product."
