## Long Directory Description

Kura helps people understand what is in the products they buy. The app lets shoppers scan food products, cosmetics, and beauty items, then turns ingredient labels into plain-language insights.

For food, Kura is built for people who want quick clarity on ingredients, additives, allergens, and product transparency without turning shopping into a research project. For cosmetics and beauty products, Kura helps users review ingredient lists and compare products more confidently.

Kura is designed as a practical label-reading tool. It is not a diet app, does not replace medical advice, and does not guarantee that a product is right for every person. It gives shoppers a faster way to inspect labels and ask better questions before they buy.
