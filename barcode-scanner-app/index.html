Barcode Scanner App

Scan product barcodes for food and beauty label context.

Lychee helps turn barcode lookups and ingredient labels into plain-language context for packaged foods, cosmetics, skincare, makeup, haircare, and other personal care products.

Two Scanner Categories

One app can help with both pantry shelves and bathroom shelves.

Food products

Scan packaged foods to review ingredient names, additive categories, allergen clues, processing signals, and source limits.

Cosmetics

Scan beauty products to understand ingredient roles, fragrance terms, preservatives, colorants, acids, and UV filters.

Label checks

Barcode data is helpful, but the package remains the source of truth when product records are missing or outdated.

How Lychee Handles Barcodes

A barcode match is the beginning of the scan, not the whole answer.

Lychee uses barcode and label context to help explain what is in a product. When the product record is incomplete, regional, outdated, or inconsistent with the package, the current package should guide your decision.

For food allergies, medical diets, skin conditions, pregnancy questions, or medication interactions, Lychee can support label reading but cannot replace qualified advice.

Good scanner behavior

The most useful barcode scanner app is transparent about uncertainty and makes it easy to keep reading beyond a single score.
