Methodology

How Kura’s Health Score Works

Kura’s Health Score turns complicated food labels into one simple number, from 0 to 100. To calculate it, we look at a product from a few different angles:

Processing

Foods that are more heavily processed usually score lower than simpler, less altered options.

Ingredients and additives

Cleaner ingredient lists tend to score better than products filled with artificial additives or industrial ingredients.

Overall food quality

We look beyond front-of-pack claims to get a more balanced picture of what’s actually inside.

Nutrition

We consider things like sugar, salt, fiber, and protein.

The result is a score designed to help you compare products quickly and make smarter everyday choices. It’s not meant to replace medical advice or tell you a food is “perfect” or “bad.” It’s simply a clearer, easier way to understand how a product stacks up at a glance.
